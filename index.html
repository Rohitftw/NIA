Who We Are

Connecting Communities, Celebrating Culture.

Since 1951, The Netherlands India Association (NIA) has served as a bridge between our two nations. We are a dedicated platform for intercultural exchange.

  • Foster diplomatic and cultural ties.
  • Organize seminars and lectures.
  • Create a high-trust community network.
Our Mission

NIA’s Objectives

Promoting knowledge and understanding of culture, history, philosophy, religion and social structure of India and The Netherlands between the people of both countries.

Promoting friendly relations between Indians and Dutch through widening and deepening personal, cultural, and social contact.

We do so by organizing a.o. programmes and symposia with a more academic, interactive content about subjects such as Indian elections, and by cultural events like musical concerts, dance performances and film shows. NIA also encourages and seeks cooperation with other organizations associated with India, with NIA functioning as “link pin”.

NIA is a networking organization where Dutch and Indians can come to know each other and can share each others knowledge and make friends.

Our Unique Identity

NIA is distinct as one of the few organizations strictly dedicated to Indo-Dutch intercultural exchange. We welcome everyone—regardless of nationality—who shares a love for our two nations.

Our Legacy

History

The Netherlands India Association (NIA) celebrates its 70th anniversary.

Driven by Purpose

Here at NIA , we are driven to do our part in making the world a better place. Since 2000, we have taken part in a wide range of activities that empower individuals and communities. We strive to build productive relationships and make a positive impact with all of our pursuits. Are you ready to join us and create real transformation in the lives of so many?

Shared Heritage

India and The Netherlands have had cultural relations for over 400 years. Rembrandt was inspired by Mughal art and there is shared Dutch-Indian heritage in present day India. Today the fast growing Indian community in the Netherlands is making an impact with their many cultural contributions.

Continuing Tradition

The Netherlands India Association (NIA) was founded to provide you with entertaining and informative insights into these two fascinating countries and its peoples. Today, we are continuing this tradition that we started since inception.
