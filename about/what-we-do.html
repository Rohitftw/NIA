About Us / What We Do
Our Approach

Type of Activities

NIA organizes different types of events such as cultural, academic and social-political programs. Programs are meant to interest people in aspects of Indian and Dutch culture in an engaging way.

Our social get-togethers generally start with an informal meet and greet, followed by the programme proper and a reception or evening program.

All events have a unique character because of its Dutch-Indian mix. Besides networking NIA strives to create an environment in which people can enjoy each other's company, background, experience and personality. Quality food and drinks – both Indian and Dutch – flavour our gatherings, whereas musical or dance performances spice up the events.

Main Programs

NIA organizes 3 major cultural events and 1 academic program, seminar or debate per year. These are:

King's Day Celebration Around 27 April
Academic Program / Debate May / June
Indian Independence Day 15 August
Diwali / Sinterklaas November

Informal Outings & Groups

In addition smaller activities are planned. These are informal outings aiming at bringing together members and guests who share the same interests. We will visit an art or photographic exhibition, see a film, visit a concert or artist studio. Everyone can bring in his or her expertise, special interest and come up with an idea.

The representative team in simple NIA aims for a short communication (Facebook, website, etc). Knowing the place, event and time, a meeting point will be given. See the Upcoming events for the dates of the main and in-between activities.

Locations

Our members come from all over the country and therefore NIA organizes its events at suitable locations in different places.

Some recent locations:
Keukenhof Lisse, Restaurant Indiaas - (Noordwijk) aan zee, Erasmus Paviljoen Rotterdam, Cobra Museum Amstelveen, Probouws The Hague, Gandhi Center The Hague, Hilton Hotel Amsterdam, Kasteel de Wittenburg Wassenaar, Spirit center in Voorschoten, Airbus Heliplaza The Hague.
