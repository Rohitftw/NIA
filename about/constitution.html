Constitution of NIA

About Us / Constitution
Contents Name, Seat, Objectives Membership Executive Board General Body Meeting Resolutions & Liquidation

Translation of the original Dutch text. (13-6-1973)

Name, Seat, Objectives

Article 1

The Association bears the name: "NETHERLANDS-INDIA ASSOCIATION" and is established in The Hague. The Association is founded for an indefinite period.

Article 2

The aim of the association is to promote the friendship, knowledge and understanding of culture, history, philosophy, religion and social structure of India and the Netherlands between the people of both countries.

Membership

Article 3

  1. The members of the Association consist of ordinary members, honorary members and donor members.
  2. Ordinary members are those who have reached the age of eighteen years and have been admitted as members by the Executive Board.
  3. Honorary members are those who, on account of their extraordinary merits to the Association or in the field of Indo-Dutch relations, have been appointed as such by the General Body Meeting on the proposal of the Executive Board.

Article 4

Membership ends by death of the member, by resignation by the member, or by resignation on behalf of the Association.

Executive Board & Advisory Council

Article 5

  1. The Executive Board consists of at least five members, including a President, a Secretary and a Treasurer.
  2. The members of the Executive Board are appointed by the General Body Meeting from the members of the Association.
  3. The President is appointed in function by the General Body Meeting.

The General Body Meeting

Article 9

Access to the General Body Meeting is open to all members of the Association who have not been suspended.

Article 10

The General Body Meetings are convened by the Executive Board. The convening notices are sent in writing to the addresses of the members according to the register referred to in Article 4.

Resolutions and Liquidation

Article 16

To amend the Constitution of the Association, a resolution of the General Body Meeting is required, which has been convened with the announcement that an amendment of the Constitution will be proposed there.

Our Sponsors

NIA.

The Netherlands India Association is a networking organization for Dutch and Indian management, research, and culture.
