About NIA Membership

NIA has three kinds of memberships i.e. Family Membership, Single Membership and Student Membership. You can opt your membership and pay online Membership Fee.

NIA "Family" Membership – Yearly

Membership Fee: €50.00 (+ Service Fee)

Family of 4 People included in Family Membership (2 Adults 2 Children Below 18 Years).

Members will get a participation in NIA Members only events.

NIA "Single" Membership – Yearly

Membership Fee: €30.00 (+ Service Fee)

One Person included in Single Membership.

NIA "Student" Membership – Yearly
